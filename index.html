1. Upload photo

JPG or PNG. Nothing leaves your browser.

2. Set scale

Click Add reference, then click 2 points on something of known height in the photo (a fence post, a door, a person). Add a second reference at a different depth so sculptures auto-shrink toward the horizon. Add more refs for better accuracy.

    Not calibrated yet.

    3. Place a sculpture

    Click a tile to add at default position, or drag a tile onto the photo to place it where you drop it. Drag a placed sculpture to reposition. Drag the blue square at the top-right corner to resize (a % tooltip shows live). Right-click for Delete / Reset size / Bring to front.
